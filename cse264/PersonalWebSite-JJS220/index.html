I was born on the 23rd of September 1998 in the town of Greenwich, CT. When I was young, I was a rather good baby despite almost getting myself killed a couple of times. I had a fascination with cars and grew a strong connection with the family cat.

School Before Lehigh

I was always a pretty good student. I went to every class and did most homeworks, even when I really didn't want to. I had a good group of friends that seemed to change every year, but I never had any regrets when it came to chosing who to spend time with. Through all my hard work in the Greenwich Public School system I managed to get admitted to Lehigh, which led me to today.

Life at Lehigh

At Lehigh I decided on the CSB major and so far have not regreted it. I have since joined the Delta Chi Fraternity and took on the role of community service chair. Today, I am a junior and secured an internship with Amazon for my future summer, which will hopefully lead to a job for my next chapter in life.
